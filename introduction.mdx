## Key Features

1. **Text Moderation**: Detect and flag inappropriate, harmful, or non-compliant content such as hate speech, profanity, and personal data leaks.
2. **Image Moderation**: Identify explicit, offensive, or otherwise restricted visual content with robust AI-powered image analysis.
3. **Information Extraction**: Extract relevant details, including flagged keywords, image tags, sentiment analysis, and metadata, for actionable insights.
4. **Customizable Rules**: Tailor moderation parameters to meet your specific requirements, ensuring precision and relevance.
5. **Real-time Performance**: Achieve rapid results with low latency, suitable for real-time applications.

## Why Choose ContentMod?

- **Scalable**: Designed to handle workloads of all sizes, from startups to enterprise-level applications.
