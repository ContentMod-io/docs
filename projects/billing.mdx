### Project-Based Billing with Stripe

Billing for ContentMod is managed seamlessly through **Stripe**, ensuring secure and transparent payment processing on a project-by-project basis.

#### How It Works:

- **Independent Billing**: Each project you create is billed separately, allowing for precise cost tracking and budgeting.
- **Secure Payments**: All transactions are processed through Stripe, a trusted global payment platform.
- **Flexible Payment Options**: Support for multiple payment methods, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and more.
- **Detailed Invoices**: Receive individual invoices for each project, making it easy to manage expenses and reconcile payments.

#### Benefits:

- Perfect for users managing multiple clients or initiatives.
- Ensures clear separation of costs across projects.
- Easy-to-monitor billing via the Stripe dashboard or our platform.

Start managing your project billing effortlessly with **Stripe's** robust and reliable payment infrastructure.
